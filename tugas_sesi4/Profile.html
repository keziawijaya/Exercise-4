Concern and love of students, especially on the students / birth of the guidance of learning A.K.A.
A.K.A learning guidance originated from 3 female programmers namely Adinda Zahra in the field of games, Amalia Fithri in the field of web,
and Kezia Wijaya in the application field, they want to learn special programming for children. This tutoring guide was established in 2016
at Lenteng Agung-Depok with the configuration by A.K.A. founder. Basically, this study guide just for home services, but with the ever-expanding
and out-of-school students the more sophisticated will the technology, then formed A.K.A.
A.K.A learning guidance is officially open itself in January 2017. Learning Guidance A.K.A has professional, friendly and competent teachers in their field each.
The guidance programs implemented vary greatly with the interests and talents of the students themselves.
